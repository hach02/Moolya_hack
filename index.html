Document readiness
Property documents
✓
Sale deed / title deed
Original + certified copy required. Ensure chain of title goes back minimum 13 years.
!
Encumbrance certificate (EC)
Get EC for last 30 years from sub-registrar office. For vacant land in Haryana, use HRERA portal. Banks will flag any prior mortgage or lien.
✗
Land use / zoning certificate
Critical for vacant land. Banks need confirmation it is non-agricultural (commercial/industrial zone). Get from DTCP Haryana. Missing this is the #1 delay cause for Faridabad plots.
!
Property tax receipts (last 3 years)
Arrears will cause rejection. Clear any outstanding dues before applying.
Business / financial documents
✓
GST returns (last 2 years)
GSTR-3B sufficient. Arka and Piramal both accept GST as primary income proof for MSME borrowers.
!
ITR with computation (last 2 years)
Ensure filed and acknowledged. Business income should show consistent trend. Large drop in Year 2 vs Year 1 will trigger a query.
✗
Business proposal / end-use letter
Required by all lenders for business purpose LAP. Must detail: business nature, how funds will be deployed, expected revenue impact, repayment source. This goes to credit committee — weak proposals add 3–4 weeks.
